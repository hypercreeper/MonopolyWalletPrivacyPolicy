Monopoly Wallet app does not collect any personal information other than error reports that are automatically sent when the app experiences an error.

Monopoly Wallet requires the following features:

  • Fine Location - Works with the Bluetooth permission to scan and connect to nearby devices to enable the send/receive money feature in the app
  • Bluetooth - Works with the location permission to scan and connect to nearby devices to enable the send/receive money features
  • Camera - The app uses this permission to enable the qr code scanner in the receive feature to be able to receive money from senders

The data from the above mentioned permissions never leave the phone to a online server, they are only used to enable local features in the app
